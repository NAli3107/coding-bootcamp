Batman Bio

Who is Batman?

Bruce Wayne is the CEO of Wayne Enterprises and the main vigilante operating in Gotham City, New Jersey known as the Batman. After witnessing the murder of his parents at the hands of a mugger as a child, Bruce waged a war on crime in Gotham City for over years before the Black Zero Event. He also serves as the leader of the Justice League which he co-founded alongside Wonder Woman.
